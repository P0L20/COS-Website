Student Organization (SROS)
Student Researchers' ORganization
Student Researchers' Organization (SRO) is a student organization in the College of Science at URS Morong that promotes a culture of research and innovation among students. It supports members in developing their research skills through seminars, workshops, and academic projects, helping them become critical thinkers and contributors to scientific knowledge.
Recently Held Activities
RISES 2026
RISES 2026 Research Congress is currently underway, showcasing student research presentations from the College of Science. Two studies from each academic program are being presented, highlighting a variety of topics, methodologies, and scientific contributions. At the end of the congress, outstanding works and presenters will be recognized through awards such as Best in Descriptive Research, Best in Experimental Research, and Best Presenter. The event celebrates research excellence and the development of strong scientific communication skills among students.
