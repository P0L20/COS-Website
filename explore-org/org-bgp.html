Student Organization (BGP)
Buklurang Gabay Patnubay
Buklurang Gabay Patnubay (BGP) is a human service organization in the College of Science at URS Morong that focuses on guiding and supporting students through outreach, counseling-related activities, and community service. It promotes compassion, leadership, and social responsibility by encouraging members to serve and uplift others both within the campus and the community.
