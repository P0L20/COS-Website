Student Organization (TIPA)
Technology Innovators Through Programming and Algorithm Sciences
Technology Innovators Through Programming and Algorithm Science (TIPA) is a student organization in the College of Science at URS Morong that focuses on developing students' skills in programming and algorithmic thinking. It promotes learning, collaboration, and innovation through activities such as coding workshops, tech talks, and projects, helping members grow into future technology professionals.
Recently Held Activities
COS E-Sports
The COS E-Sports event, facilitated by TIPA Org, brought together students to compete in organized gaming tournaments. The activity aimed to promote teamwork, sportsmanship, and healthy competition among participants while showcasing their skills in e-sports.
