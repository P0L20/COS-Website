Student Organization (SMS)
Samahan ng mga Mag-aaral sa Sikolohiya
Samahan ng mga Mag-aaral sa Sikolohiya (SMS) is a student organization in the College of Science at URS Morong that brings together psychology students to promote awareness, understanding, and appreciation of human behavior and mental processes. It organizes academic activities, seminars, and community programs that support personal growth, mental health advocacy, and professional development.
