Student Organization (The ESSENCE)
The Essence
The Essence is the official publication group of the College of Science at URS Morong. It focuses on delivering news, feature stories, and creative works that highlight student voices, achievements, and issues within the college, while promoting responsible journalism and campus awareness.
Recently Held Activities
Journalism Competition
The Journalism Competition, organized by the ESSENCE Org, brought together students to showcase their skills in writing, reporting, and critical thinking. Participants competed in various categories that tested their ability to deliver clear, accurate, and impactful stories. The event highlighted creativity and journalistic excellence among aspiring campus writers. Overall, it aimed to strengthen communication skills and promote responsible journalism among students.
