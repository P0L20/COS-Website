BS in Computer Science
What do we do on Computer Science?

BS Computer Science (BSCS) is a program focused on the study of computing, software development, and problem-solving using technology. It covers key areas such as programming, algorithms, data structures, databases, and artificial intelligence. Students develop strong analytical and technical skills through hands-on projects and system design. The program prepares graduates for careers in software engineering, IT, data science, and other technology-driven fields.
