BS in Psychology
What do we do on Psychology?

BS Psychology is a program focused on the study of human behavior, thoughts, emotions, and mental processes. It covers key areas such as developmental psychology, abnormal psychology, cognitive psychology, social psychology, and research methods. Students develop strong analytical, observational, and research skills through experiments, case studies, and psychological assessments. The program prepares graduates for careers in mental health services, education, human resources, counseling, and other people-centered professions.
