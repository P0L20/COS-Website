BS in Human Services
What do we do on Human Services?

BS Human Services is a program focused on helping individuals, families, and communities improve their well-being and quality of life. It covers key areas such as counseling, social work, community development, mental health support, and case management. Students develop strong communication, empathy, and problem-solving skills through fieldwork, outreach programs, and practical training. The program prepares graduates for careers in social services, counseling, community organizations, and other helping professions.
