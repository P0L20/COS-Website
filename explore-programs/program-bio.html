BS in Biology
What do we do on Biology?

BS Biology is a program focused on the study of living organisms, their structures, functions, growth, and interactions with the environment. It covers key areas such as cell biology, genetics, ecology, microbiology, and evolution. Students develop strong analytical and laboratory skills through hands-on experiments, research, and fieldwork. The program prepares graduates for careers in healthcare, research, environmental science, biotechnology, and other life science–related fields.
