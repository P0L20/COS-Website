About the College of Science

The College of Science at URS Morong offers quality education and fosters innovation in various scientific fields. It is committed to developing skilled and future-ready graduates through strong academic programs and practical learning. The college offers Bachelor of Science programs in Mathematics, Computer Science, Biology, History, and Psychology.

What Sets the College of Science Apart
  • Competent Faculty
    Highly qualified and experienced professors dedicated to delivering quality education and mentorship in various scientific fields.
  • Innovative
    The college promotes cutting-edge research that addresses real-world problems and contributes to scientific advancement.
  • Future-Ready Graduates
    Equipping students with critical thinking, technical skills, and adaptability needed for modern scientific careers.
  • Student Organizations
    Active communities fostering growth and collaboration
